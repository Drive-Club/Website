Economy System

Learn about the economy, trading, currency, and resource management in DeadZone Chronicles.

Currency System

The economy in DeadZone Chronicles is primarily resource-based, with items and materials serving as the main form of currency.

Resources as Currency

Most trading is done through barter using resources and items:

  • Wood and Stone - Basic building materials
  • Metal and Components - Advanced crafting materials
  • Weapons and Gear - High-value trade items
  • Medical Supplies - Essential survival items
  • Rare Items - Premium trade currency

Trading System

Players can trade with each other directly or through various methods:

  • Direct Trading: Face-to-face trades with other players
  • Drop Trading: Place items on ground for pickup (risky)
  • Base Trading: Secure trading areas within bases
  • Marketplace: Central trading hub (coming soon)
  • Auction System: Bid on rare items (coming soon)

⚠️ Warning: Trading with other players is done at your own risk. Be cautious of scams and always verify trades before completing them.

Resource Management

Gathering Resources

  • Wood - Harvested from trees and wooden structures
  • Stone - Mined from rocks and stone deposits
  • Metal - Found in scrap piles and vehicles
  • Cloth - Collected from clothing and fabric items
  • Components - Scavenged from electronics and machinery

Resource Storage

  • Store resources in containers within your base
  • Use multiple storage locations for security
  • Organize resources by type for easy access
  • Keep valuable resources well-protected

Resource Value

Resource values fluctuate based on:

  • Server economy and supply/demand
  • Rarity and difficulty to obtain
  • Usefulness in crafting and building
  • Current meta and player preferences

Item Economy

Item Rarity Tiers

  • Common: Basic items, easy to find
  • Uncommon: Standard items, moderate rarity
  • Rare: Valuable items, harder to obtain
  • Epic: High-tier items, very rare
  • Legendary: Extremely rare, highly valuable

High-Value Items

  • Rare weapons and attachments
  • Advanced armor sets
  • Mystery box exclusive items
  • Dungeon boss rewards
  • Event-exclusive cosmetics

Future Economy Features

The following economy features are planned for future updates:

  • Dedicated currency system
  • Central marketplace and trading hub
  • Auction house for rare items
  • NPC traders and shops
  • Economic events and market fluctuations
  • Player-run shops and businesses
