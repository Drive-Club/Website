Gameplay Overview

DeadZone Chronicles combines the best elements of survival, PvP, and zombie apocalypse gameplay. Players spawn into a post-apocalyptic wasteland where they must gather resources, build bases, craft equipment, and fight both zombies and other players to survive.

The server features full PvP combat everywhere, meaning you're never safe. Base raiding is a core mechanic, so players must build and fortify their bases carefully. The world is filled with loot locations, zombie spawns, and mystery boxes to discover.

Whether you prefer solo play or teaming up with others, DeadZone Chronicles offers a challenging and rewarding survival experience. Form clans, raid enemy bases, or simply survive in the wasteland - the choice is yours.
