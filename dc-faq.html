Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about DeadZone Chronicles. Can't find what you're looking for? Join our Discord and ask!

General Questions

What is DeadZone Chronicles?
DeadZone Chronicles is a zombie apocalypse survival server with full PvP combat, base building, raiding, crafting, looting, and more. It combines elements of Rust-style gameplay with zombie survival mechanics.
When will the server be released?
The release date is currently TBD. Join our Discord to stay updated on development progress and release announcements.
Is the server free to play?
Yes, DeadZone Chronicles is free to play. There may be optional cosmetic purchases in the future, but core gameplay will always be free.
What platform is this on?
DeadZone Chronicles is a FiveM server for Grand Theft Auto V. You'll need GTA V and FiveM installed to play.

Gameplay Questions

Is PvP enabled everywhere?
Yes, PvP is enabled everywhere on the server. There are no safe zones, so always be prepared for combat.
Can I build bases?
Yes! Base building is a core feature. You can construct and fortify your base using various materials and building components.
Can other players raid my base?
Yes, base raiding is a core gameplay mechanic. Players can raid your base using explosives and other raiding tools. Make sure to fortify your base well!
What happens when I die?
When you die, you'll drop your inventory items. Other players can loot your body. You'll respawn at a spawn point, but you'll need to recover your gear if possible.
How does the crafting system work?
You can craft weapons, tools, armor, medical supplies, and building materials using resources you gather from the world. Crafting recipes unlock as you progress.
What are mystery boxes?
Mystery boxes are special containers that can be found or earned that contain rare items, weapons, cosmetics, and exclusive rewards. They add an element of chance and reward to gameplay.

Technical Questions

What are the server requirements?
You need Grand Theft Auto V installed and FiveM. The server requirements match GTA V's system requirements. A stable internet connection is recommended for the best experience.
Will there be server wipes?
Periodic server wipes may occur to keep the gameplay fresh and balanced. Wipe schedules will be announced in advance on Discord.
Is there a whitelist?
Whitelist status will be announced closer to release. Join our Discord to stay updated on whitelist applications and requirements.

Community Questions

How do I join the Discord?
You can join our Discord server by clicking the Discord button on the main page or visiting: https://discord.gg/fF7wU3f6fN
How do I report bugs or issues?
Report bugs and issues through our Discord ticket system. Include as much detail as possible, including screenshots or videos if applicable.
Can I apply to be staff?
Staff applications will open closer to release. Keep an eye on Discord announcements for when applications become available.
How do I appeal a ban?
Ban appeals can be submitted through the proper Discord channel. Be respectful and provide any relevant information. Staff decisions are final.
