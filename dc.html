Server Rules

Rules and guidelines for all players

1. Respect All Players
Treat all players with respect. Harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior will not be tolerated.
2. No Cheating or Exploiting
Any form of cheating, hacking, or exploiting game mechanics is strictly prohibited and will result in a permanent ban.
3. PvP Guidelines
PvP is allowed everywhere. However, excessive spawn camping or targeting new players repeatedly may result in warnings.
4. Base Raiding
Base raiding is permitted, but excessive griefing or destroying bases without purpose is discouraged.
5. No Advertising
Advertising other servers, services, or products is not allowed in-game or on Discord.
6. Follow Discord Rules
All Discord community guidelines must be followed. Discord moderators have final say.
7. Report Issues
Report bugs, exploits, or rule violations through proper channels. False reports may result in action.
8. Staff Decisions Are Final
All staff decisions regarding rules, bans, or server management are final. Arguing with staff will not be tolerated.
